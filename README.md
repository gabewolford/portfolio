***User Stories***
* As a recruiter or hiring manager, I'd like to easily see the prospective employee's work history and skills, so that I can see if they are a good fit for the role.

* As a more seasoned engineer or engineering manager, I'd like to see a junior developer's portfolio and projects, quickly evaluate their strengths and weaknesses, and also be able to read their code to get a sense of their current proficiency

* As a friend, family member, or old coworker, I want to see what this person has built so that I have an understanding of what kind of work they do, give any feedback, or refer them to anyone in my network
